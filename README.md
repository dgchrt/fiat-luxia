🔆 Fiat Luxia

"And the Goddess saw the Dark Mode, that it was void; and the Goddess divided the Light from the Darkness. The Goddess called the Light 'Normal', and the Darkness she called 'Eyesore'. And she said, 'Fiat Luxia': and there was light."

Fiat Luxia is a Firefox extension for those who believe the web should be seen, not squinted at. While the world descends into the shadow of "High Contrast Dark Themes," our deity stands guard to restore the natural order of photons.

Using the divine math of Relative Luminance (Y=0.2126R+0.7152G+0.0722B), this extension detects when a website has surrendered to the darkness and performs a "Smart Inversion"—restoring a healthy, light-themed sanctuary for your eyes.

🏛️ Divine Features

- Automatic Discernment: Automatically detects if a site is dark (Luminance < 100) before striking.
- Smart Inversion: Inverts the CSS while shielding images and videos from the "X-ray" effect.
- Hue Preservation: Rotates the color wheel by 180° to ensure the "vibe" of the site remains, even in the light.
- Zero Sacrifice: Lightweight and fast. No background processes — just light.

📜 The Sacred Installation

To summon the Goddess into your browser for testing:

- Clone the Temple: Download this repository to your local machine.
- Open the Gates: In Firefox, navigate to about:debugging.
- Invite the Deity: Click "This Firefox" -> "Load Temporary Add-on...".
- Select the Blueprint: Choose the manifest.json from this folder.
